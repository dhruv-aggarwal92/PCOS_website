How to Use the PCOS Prediction Form

To detect the likelihood of PCOS using our machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL) models, we require you to fill in the following form with accurate personal information and symptom details. Here's how each section contributes to the prediction:

  • Personal Information: Basic data like age, height, and weight help calculate your BMI, which is a significant factor in predicting PCOS.
  • Symptoms: Inputting your symptoms like irregular cycles, skin darkening, excessive hair growth, and pimples gives our model vital information about hormonal imbalances that are commonly associated with PCOS.
  • Body Measurements: Waist and hip measurements allow us to calculate the Waist-to-Hip Ratio (WHR), which is another indicator for PCOS-related conditions.
  • Ultrasound Image: If you have an ultrasound image, uploading it (optional) helps our model analyze ovarian structures, which is another common sign of PCOS.

Once all fields are filled out, click the "Submit Prediction" button. Our ML and DL models will process the data, analyze patterns, and provide you with a prediction along with a confidence score. Please ensure the information provided is accurate for the best results.
